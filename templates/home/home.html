Move Forward.
Our team draws from the most advanced methods available to treat root causes of substance abuse and mental health issues. Many clients arrive at our facility after years of unsuccessful therapy and addiction treatment and report an immediate sense that something unique and powerful is available. Our approach combines cutting-edge techniques from the fields of neuroscience, psychology, pharmacology, traditional and alternative medicine, physiology, and the creative arts. Our goal is not only to get you unstuck but to keep you that way, so you move forward with the life you desire. Our evidence-based treatment model, expert clinical team, and safe & comfortable environment help you learn how to live your life – free from addiction.
Get Unstuck.
The Tree House – our rustic and peaceful Residential Treatment Center– is a place for you to build a solid foundation of recovery and wellness in an atmosphere of learning and change. Nestled in the hills near downtown Los Gatos, the Tree House has 6 private or shared rooms where you can rest, repair and restore your mind, body, and soul. Residential activities are flexible and adaptable while providing the real-life tools and skills you need to live and thrive in recovery and beyond. Clients report a sense of peace and excitement to be finally moving forward and addressing the underlying causes of chronic issues. When it's time to get unstuck, call or email us to discuss how the Tree House can be your next best step towards long-term recovery.

Why Our Program Works
Our team draws from the most advanced methods available to treat root causes of substance abuse and mental health issues. Many clients arrive at our facility after years of unsuccessful therapy and addiction treatment and report an immediate sense that something unique and powerful is available.
Unique Therapy
This cutting edge treatment utilizes the best of what neuroscience recommends for resolving deeply embedded reactivity. You will experience pain-free, uplifting techniques designed to quickly and effectively rewrite core beliefs and unhook painful emotions from your past.

Life Skills
Life is demanding. Getting sober can be challenging. Staying sober is a skill! We will teach you a variety of solution-focused, skill building techniques to help you navigate career, relationship and transitional life issues calmly and effectively. 12-Step option available.



Alumni Maintenence
Congratulations to all who get and stay sober in this challenging world! We welcome all alumni to attend groups and events (as long as there is an extra chair) to continue learning, growing, and giving back to others. Join in, volunteer, make a difference. You are part of our tribe.
What it all costs
We accept insurance and private pay. Fees and payment options depend on many factors. We will work with you as much as possible to ensure you can get the help you need at a price you can afford. Call to discuss which program and financial option works best for you.
Custom Plan
One size does not fit all: We create unique, individualized treatment programs based on the patient’s specific needs. Our 24/7 medically-supervised detoxification ensures our patients detox both comfortably and safely.
